About Me

Born and mostly raised in Indianapolis, Indiana and soon to be resident of Chicago, Illinois.

I attended Indiana University in Bloomington, Indiana from 2007-2011 where I graduated with an undergraduate degree in economics. Upon graduating I worked a couple finance related jobs in Chicago and Indianapolis until this past February.

My main hobby is traveling and experiencing other cultures. My two friends and I had left ours jobs this past winter to travel all over the world and were able to experience 15 countries. My main highlights from all of the traveling that I’ve done over the past few years include: snowmobiling glaciers in Iceland, running with the bulls, scuba diving in Zanzibar, and volunteering with earthquake relief efforts in Nepal.

My biggest quirk about would be when I’m not wearing shoes, I typical am only wearing one sock on one foot and the other barefoot.
